About Liquid

Liquid is a strategy game. Inspired by games like Reversi, where vast parts of the board can flip from one player's to another's control, Liquid features common reversals of large portions of the board from one player to another.

Liquid comes in two editions: the standard edition, for all browsers, and the developer edition which may run faster and lends to better debugging, but only works on recent versions of Firefox.
